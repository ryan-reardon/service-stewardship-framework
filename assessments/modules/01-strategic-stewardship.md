# Domain Module: Strategic Stewardship (Deep Dive)

## Strategy & Alignment
- Roadmap aligned to enterprise strategy
- Investment thesis documented
- Lifecycle stage defined and reviewed

## Value Realization
- Outcome metrics defined before execution
- Post-delivery validation performed
- Cost-to-value tracked quarterly

## Modernization Governance
- Modernization capacity protected
- Debt reduction linked to roadmap
- Sunset criteria defined

## Stakeholder Engagement
- Stakeholder map maintained
- Quarterly strategic review held
- Trade-offs communicated clearly

Key Metrics:
- Adoption growth
- Roadmap confidence
- Cost-to-value trend
